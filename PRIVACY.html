Privacy Notice

At Nomads Nook, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Notice outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services. By using Nomads Nook, you consent to the practices described in this notice.

1. Information We Collect:

We collect different types of information from you when you visit or use our platform, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details provided during account creation or communication with property owners.
  • Property Information: Details about the property you list, such as address, features, pricing, and photos.
  • Payment Information: For processing rental payments or application fees, we may collect financial information like credit card details. This data is securely processed by our payment partners.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, including browsing behavior, pages visited, and time spent on the site, collected via cookies and similar technologies.

2. How We Use Your Information:

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Facilitating transactions and communications between renters and property owners.
  • Providing customer support and responding to inquiries.
  • Verifying identity and processing rental applications.
  • Improving our platform and personalizing your experience.
  • Sending updates, notifications, and promotional materials (you can opt out of marketing communications at any time).
  • Ensuring compliance with legal obligations and protecting against fraud or security risks.

3. Sharing Your Information:

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that assist in operating our platform (e.g., payment processors, hosting services) and are required to protect your data.
  • Property Owners: When you express interest in a property, your contact information is shared with the owner or agent.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights, we may disclose information to government authorities or other relevant parties.

4. Data Security:

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to take precautions to safeguard your own information.

5. Your Data Rights:

You have rights concerning your personal information, including the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.
  • Opt out of receiving marketing communications.
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@nomadsnook.com.

6. Changes to This Privacy Notice:

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated notice on our website. Your continued use of the platform after these changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
